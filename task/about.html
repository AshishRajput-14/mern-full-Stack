About the Shopping Cart

.An online shopping cart is an essential feature of an e-commerce website that allows users to add, view, update, and remove products before completing a purchase. It works like a digital shopping basket where product details such as name, price, quantity, and total cost are stored temporarily. The cart automatically updates the total price based on quantity changes, helping users clearly understand the cost before checkout. In this project, the shopping cart is developed using JavaScript for dynamic functionality, Bootstrap for responsive design, and localStorage to retain cart data even after page refresh. Overall, it enhances user experience by making online shopping smooth, flexible, and convenient.
